Components #back to top

Container - #back to top

A container based on neumorphism. It has the following properties.

  1. type - Either 'flat', 'convex', 'concave' or 'pressed'. Please refer over here for what they each mean in more details.
  2. borderRadius - The border radius of your container.
  3. blurStrength - The blur strength of the container.
  4. backgroundColor - The background colour of your container, make sure that this is the same as the one below it, for maximum neumorphism effect.
  5. width - The width of the container.
  6. height - The height of the container.
  7. distance - The distance to which the shadow extends.
  8. innerElementAlignItems - Controls the alignment of the top level tag of the children included in the container.
  9. innerElementJustifyContent - Just like innerElementAlignItems, this deals with the top level tag, but the justifyContent property instead.
  10. innerShadowPosition - Controls the position of the inner shadow of the container. This can be either the 4 mentioned below:
    1. lt - Left Top
    2. lb - Left Bottom
    3. rt - Right Top
    4. rb - Right Bottom
  11. outerShadowPosition - Similar to the inner shadow position but deals with the outer area.
  12. innerElementPadding - The padding that gives to the inner children

Button - #back to top

Button that toggle between either pressed state or bubbly state.

  1. type - Same as above, but it is recommended to keep it the default of 'convex', since on click the type changes between either 'convex' or 'concave', with respect to click
  2. borderRadius - The border radius of your button.
  3. blurStrength - The blur strength of the button.
  4. backgroundColor - The background colour of your button, make sure that this is the same as the one below it, for maximum neumorphism effect.
  5. width - The width of the button.
  6. height - The height of the button.
  7. distance - The distance to which the shadow extends.
  8. innerElementAlignItems - Controls the alignment of the top level tag of the children included in the button.
  9. innerElementJustifyContent - Just like innerElementAlignItems, this deals with the top level tag, but the justifyContent property instead.
  10. innerShadowPosition - Controls the position of the inner shadow of the button. This can be either the 4 mentioned below:
    1. lt - Left Top
    2. lb - Left Bottom
    3. rt - Right Top
    4. rb - Right Bottom
  11. outerShadowPosition - Similar to the inner shadow position but deals with the outer area.
  12. innerElementPadding - The padding that gives to the inner children.
  13. onClickFunc - A method that executes when the button is clicked.

Button Group - #back to top

Contains 3 button group that combines the magic of neumorphism into one.

  1. type - Same as above, but it is recommended to keep it the default of 'convex', since on click the type changes between either 'convex' or 'concave', with respect to click
  2. borderRadius - The radius of your button group.
  3. blurStrength - The blur strength of the button group.
  4. backgroundColor - The background colour of your button group, make sure that this is the same as the one below it, for maximum neumorphism effect.
  5. width - The width of each of the buttons in the button group
  6. height - The height of each of the buttons in the button group
  7. distance - Distance of the shadow.
  8. innerElementAlignItems - Controls the alignment of the top level tag of the children included in the button group.
  9. innerElementJustifyContent - Just like innerElementAlignItems, this deals with the top level tag, but the justifyContent property instead.
  10. innerShadowPosition - Controls the position of the inner shadow of the button group. This can be either the 4 mentioned below:
    1. lt - Left Top
    2. lb - Left Bottom
    3. rt - Right Top
    4. rb - Right Bottom
  11. outerShadowPosition - Similar to the inner shadow position but deals with the outer area.
  12. innerElementPadding - The padding that gives to the inner children.
  13. firstButton - The component that is inscribed in the first button in the button group.
  14. secondButton - The component that is inscribed in the second button in the button group.
  15. thirdButton - The component that is inscribed in the third button in the button group.
  16. onClickFunc - A method that executes when one of the button is clicked.

Switch - #back to top

Switch that embraces the beauty of neumorphism in whole.

  1. isOn - Indicates whether the switch is on or off.
  2. handleToggle - The method that executes upon switching.
  3. blurStrength - The blur strength of the radio button.
  4. borderRadius - The border radius of the radio button.
  5. backgroundColor - The background colour of your button, make sure that this is the same as the one below it, for maximum neumorphism effect.
  6. distance - Distance of the shadow.
  7. width - The width of the radio button.
  8. height - The height of the radio button.
  9. innerShadowPosition - Controls the position of the inner shadow of the radio button. This can be either the 4 mentioned below:
    1. lt - Left Top
    2. lb - Left Bottom
    3. rt - Right Top
    4. rb - Right Bottom
  10. outerShadowPosition - Similar to the inner shadow position but deals with the outer area.
